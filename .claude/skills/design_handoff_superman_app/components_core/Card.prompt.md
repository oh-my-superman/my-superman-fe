Warm surface container with optional header/footer parts.

```jsx
<Card>
  <CardHeader>
    <CardTitle>세이프워드</CardTitle>
    <CardDescription>이 문장을 말하면 위급 모드로 전환돼요.</CardDescription>
  </CardHeader>
  <CardContent>"우산 가져갈게"</CardContent>
</Card>
```

`flat` drops the shadow; `inset` gives a borderless surface fill for grouped settings rows.
